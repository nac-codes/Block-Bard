- Use "CULTURAL PROPOSAL:" to suggest new discussion topics

1. How cultural changes affected economic and military decisions
2. The reciprocal relationship between cultural values and political/economic structures
3. The cultural impact of military and economic changes
4. How different groups interpreted and responded to cultural shifts
5. The role of cultural integration in both strengthening and weakening the empire

- Propose new ways to understand the interaction of different factors
- Use position structure to track how the debate evolves
